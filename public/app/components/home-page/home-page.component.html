What is PollEase?

PollEase is a web application that helps anyone to easily be their own event planner. We aim to help anyone collect information on their invitees prefered time and location preferences, in a fast and non intrusive way. Get started now, it only takes 3 minutes and an email to create and send out your first poll, and seconds for invitees to submit their vote.
